TRAFFIC:Traffic is defined as vehicles or people in the vehicles, a delay that involves transportation or movement through a designated location. An example of traffic is people driving in cars. An example of traffic is a heavy delay in a railroad system. An example of traffic is how many hits a website gets daily. Here, we will be talking about the traffic on roads.

TRAFFIC MANGEMENT: Traffic Management refers to the combination of measures that serve to preserve traffic capacity and improve the security, safety and reliability of the overall road transport system. These measures make use of ITS systems, services and projects in day-to-day operations that impact on road network performance.

Rules

The google defination of rules is 'one of a set of explicit or understood regulations or principles governing conduct or procedure within a particular area of activity'. It basically means everything we do has limits and requirements. We will be explaining to you the rules of the road.

Rules of the Road

Again, the google defination of rules of the road is 'a custom or law regulating the direction in which two vehicles (or riders or ships) should move to pass one another on meeting, or which should yield to the other, so as to avoid collision.' This basically means that we should follow rules to avoid accidents while driving a vehicle. Some of the most common rules are:

1. KEEP A LOOKOUT FOR SIGNS: The signs can specify various things-from speed limits to signifying a bump in the road-

2.KEEP VEHICLE FIT: To prevent breakdown and accidents on road.

3.NEVER USE MOBILE WHILE DRIVING:To avoid distractions that largely lead to accidents.

4.WEAR HELMET: To protect your head while riding a two wheeler. A good quality helmet reduces the chances of severe head injury.
