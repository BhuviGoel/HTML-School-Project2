United Kingdom-Traffic Rules and Violations

UNITED KINDOM (UK)

Traffic Rules:

1. The UK follows left-handed traffic
2. The average speed limit is 30mph
3. Wearing seatbelts and/or helmets are compulsory
4. Making way for emergency vehicles are mandatory
5. Pedestrians must keep to the right while walking
6. Pedestrians must not use the motorways or slip roads unless it is an emergency
7. Jumping red lights is not allowed
8.Any citizen of age 15 years and 9 months old can apply for a driving license
9. Driving license, Insurance and pollution check is required to drive a vehicle on the road
10. If traffic is approaching from your right, you must give way to the vehicle unless you are directed to do otherwise by signs, road markings or traffic lights.
